Who We Are

Nurturing Young Dragons

At 3150 Dragons Soccer Academy, we're passionate about nurturing the love for soccer in young talents aged 6 to 12. Our goal is to develop their soccer skills and instil values of teamwork, discipline, and sportsmanship.

We believe every child deserves a supportive environment where they can grow, have fun, and discover their full potential — both on the pitch and in life.

Based right here in Wheelers Hill, we're your local soccer community. Come down, give it a try, and become part of the Dragons family.
